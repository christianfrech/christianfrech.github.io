    • Link between volume change in electrochemical decomposition reactions & accelerated dendrite growth

    Recent works have proposed that a “stress-corrosion cracking-like” process worsens the fracture resistance of SEs and facilitates Li dendrite growth in solid state batteries. In the proposed process, nascent SE exposed by the advancing dendrite re- acts with metal from the dendrite. The volume change associated with this reaction increases the local stress, fostering SE fracture and dendrite propagation. Here, first- principles calculations are used to predict the volume changes associated with these M/SE (M = Li, Na) reactions as a function of electrochemical potential. These incorporate workflows from the materials project using the pymatgen APIs to calculate expected decomposition reactions & pathways.
